Odin's Table: Viking Bread Recipe

Image and Recipe Source: GlobalKitchenTravels.com

This recipe from GlobalKitchenTravels.com makes a hearty Viking Bread Loaf. Topped with oats and made with whole wheat flower gives any viking the energy to raid or make it well through a whole work day.

This recipe yields one loaf:

Details

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 45 minutes
  • Total time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/3 cup oats + 1 tablespoon

Instructions

  1. Combine flours, baking soda and salt.
  2. Add oats and water. Mix until you have a shaggy dough.
  3. Transfer to a lightly floured surface and knead until the dough comes together.
  4. Transfer to a cookie sheet.
  5. Brush the top with a little water and add the remaining oats on top.
  6. Bake in a 375 F oven for 45 minutes.
